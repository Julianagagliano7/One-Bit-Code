Galaxy Guide

Andromeda Galaxy

Description

The Andromeda Galaxy (Messier 31, NGC 224) is a spiral galaxy located about 2.54 million light-years away from Earth, in the direction of the Andromeda constellation

Interesting facts

  1. Besides the Milky Way, it is the most studied galaxy
  2. Its two satellite galaxies, Messier 32 and Messier 110, are visible in binoculars
  3. Its distance relative to Earth has not yet been well defined

Other galaxys: Black Eye, Large Magellanic Cloud
