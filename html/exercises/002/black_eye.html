Black Eye Galaxy

Description

The Black Eye Galaxy (Messier 64, NGC 4826) is a spiral galaxy located approximately 17 million light-years (about 5.2 megaparsecs) away in the direction of the constellation Coma Berenices.

Interesting facts

  1. Scientists believe in the hypothesis that it is the result of the collision between two galaxies
  2. The spiral galaxy was discovered by Edward Pigott on March 23, 1779
  3. It can be seen with good binoculars

    4. Other galaxys: Andromeda, Large Magellanic Cloud
