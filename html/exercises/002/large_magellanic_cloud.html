Large Magellanic Cloud Galaxy

Description

The Large Magellanic Cloud (LMC) is a dwarf satellite galaxy that orbits around the Milky Way.

Interesting facts

  1. It is rich in gas and dust
  2. It was named after Ferdinand Magellan
  3. The Large Magellanic Cloud is one of the closest galaxies to the Milky Way

Other galaxys: Andromeda, Black Eye
